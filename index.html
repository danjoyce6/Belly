Many bacterial species have not been studied. The goal of this research study was to find bacteria that could match the taste of beef.
A lot of research has been done, but that bacteria has not been found yet. There are thousands of species of bacteria that can be found on the human body
and inside of it. The navels of individuals across the country were sampled and assigned an ID number in the hopes of finding that bacteria. The following visualizations represent
the results of the study for each individual including the types of bacteria found and their frequency, the species of bacteria and amount
per sample, and the frequency at which each individual scrubs their belly button per week.
